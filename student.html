Hello!

I'm Sakke, a Seattle-born multimedia journalist and current Senior at Northwestern University studying journalism, psychology, and design. During my time here I've had the chance to be the creative director for multiple student magazines, work as a graphic designer for Northwestern's Student Affairs marketing team, "study abroad" in San Francisco to learn UX design and data visualization skills, and produce print and digital design pieces as an intern at National Geographic.

Ultimately, I love finding new ways to solve design problems, bring stories or brands to life, and engage audiences. It is my dream to work in a fast-paced and collaborative work environment where I can continue to learn and grow as a visual storyteller. I also believe journalism and design have the power to create meaningful change, so it is my personal goal to use my skills to work with and support marginalized communities.

In my free time I'm passionate about tackling people on the rugby pitch, capturing photos of my surroundings, learning how to cook Ethiopian food with my mom, testing my binge-watching abilities, and advocating for the oxford comma.
